Carbonara

This Carbonara Recipe is easy to make

This easy Pasta Carbonara comes together in about 30 minutes and is the perfect weeknight dinner! Packed with crispy bacon and mixed with the creamiest carbonara sauce, this recipe is a total knockout.

Ingridients

  • Bacon
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Spaghetti or linguine
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Add bacon and water to a skillet and bring to a simmer.
  2. Continue simmering until water is evaporated, then continue to cook the bacon until crispy.
  3. Remove bacon from pan and reserve the drippings.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti or linguine pasta until al dente. Once cooked, drain pasta and reserve 1 cup of the cooking water.
  5. Slowly pour the hot cooking water into the egg mixture. Then pour over the hot pasta and toss to coat. Add crumbled bacon.
  6. Let pasta rest for a few minutes, tossing frequently until the carbonara sauce thickens. Serve immediately with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.
